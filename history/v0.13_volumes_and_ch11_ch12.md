## Feedback items (directives)

### 1. Three-volume structure

The book is divided into three volumes using `\part{}` with `\partname` set to
"Volume":

- **Volume I** (Ch 1–12): Core CT foundations, no connections to other fields.
- **Volume II** (Ch 13–24): Recapitulation at ~half familiarity; chapter ordering
  rotated; arguments use two-column `prooflog` (step | justification) format.
- **Volume III** (Ch 25–36): High-familiarity; formal/terse; advanced constructions.

### 2. Stop and push after each volume

After completing Volume I, commit and push. Repeat after Volume II, then Volume III.

### 3. Exercise spacing: 3× vertical gap + thin horizontal rule

`\qaitem` updated: vertical spacing increased from `\\[0.3em]` to approximately
`\\[0.9em]` (split as `0.3em` before rule + `0.6em` after), with a 0.3pt
horizontal rule in `DialogBorder!50` between each Q/A pair.

### 4. Volume I completes core CT — no connections

Ch 11 and Ch 12 complete the foundational arc. Connections to other mathematical
fields (algebra, topology, logic, homotopy) are deferred beyond Volume III.

### preamble.tex additions
