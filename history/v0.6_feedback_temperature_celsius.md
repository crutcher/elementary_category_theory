# v0.6 — Decision: Temperature Notation → Celsius

**Date:** 2026-05-11
**Covers:** Ch 1 §1.2 Exp 1 temperature example (see v0.2)

---

## Decision

Use **Celsius**. Replace the bare degree values with:

| Old | New |
|-----|-----|
| `$32°$` | `$0°\text{C}$` |
| `$68°$` | `$20°\text{C}$` |
| `$98°$` | `$37°\text{C}$` |
| `$212°$` | `$100°\text{C}$` |

Mnemonic anchors: freezing, room temperature, body temperature, boiling.
All four are widely recognized and non-US-centric.

The surrounding prose ("is colder than" ordering) requires no other changes.

**Status:** Ready to apply in next book-edit batch.
